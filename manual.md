- ## links

    ##### To create a link, enclose the link text in brackets (e.g., [Yandex]) and then follow it immediately with the URL in parentheses (e.g., (https://ya.ru)).

    
    *Example 1:*
        
        Alice is cute. I love [Alice](https://alice.yandex.ru). 

    *Result:* 
        
    Alice is cute. I love [Alice](https://alice.yandex.ru).
